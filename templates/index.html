The Complete Guide to GPS Updates

Keeping your GPS device updated is essential for accurate navigation, smarter route planning, and up-to-date information.

Handheld GPS Updates

Simply remove your GPS device from your vehicle, connect it to your computer with a USB cable, and install the latest maps in just a few clicks.

In-Dash GPS Updates

Remove the SD card from your in-dash GPS system, insert it into a card reader, and connect it to your computer to download the latest maps.

Bike GPS Updates

Remove your GPS device from your bike, connect it to your computer with a USB cable, and easily install the newest map updates.

Marine GPS Updates

Take the SD card from your marine GPS, insert it into a card reader, and connect it to your computer. Download the latest marine charts for safe navigation.

Golf Watch Updates

Connect your Golf Buddy to your computer using a USB cable. Update your golf course maps for accurate navigation on every round.

GPS Map Updates Made Simple

Connect your GPS device to your computer, open your GPS management software, and follow the on-screen instructions to install the latest map updates.

GPS Update Process

1

Plug-In / Connect Your Device
