The Complete Guide to GPS Updates

Keeping your GPS device updated is essential for accurate navigation, smarter route planning, and up-to-date information on new roads and points of interest. This all-in-one guide will take you step-by-step through the update process, covering a variety of GPS models and brands.

Easily Update Your Handheld GPS

Simply remove your GPS device from your vehicle, connect it to your computer with a USB cable, and install the latest maps in just a few clicks.

Easily Update Your In-Dash GPS

Remove the SD card from your in-dash GPS system, insert it into a card reader, and connect it to your computer. Download and install the latest maps easily.
